Questions?

Frequently asked questions.

What do I need to bring?

Your laptop and charger. Windows or Mac, either works. Create a free ChatGPT account (chat.openai.com) before you arrive — saves us 15 minutes.

I'm not technical at all. Will I keep up?

Yes. The workshop assumes zero technical background. You'll follow step-by-step guidance and build everything with live support from the facilitators.

Is this in-person or virtual?

In-person. Novare Mall, Abuja. 10AM – 4PM. The hands-on format doesn't work well over video — you'll have facilitators walking the room.

What's the refund policy?

Full refund up to 7 days before the workshop. After that, you can transfer your spot to someone else — just let us know.

Can I come with a colleague or teammate?

Yes. Encourage them to register separately so we can account for numbers. You'll likely get paired together for the peer matching stage.

What happens after the workshop?

You get a 7-day implementation checklist to keep momentum, access to the WhatsApp community for support, and a 1-month check-in from us to ensure the workflows are running.

Is there a certificate?

Yes — you'll receive a certificate of completion from Stones and Stacks Consulting.
