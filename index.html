About Us

Knowledge that endures.

We are a collective of practitioners turned educators, united by a conviction that the knowledge economy belongs to those who build it.

Our Mission

Equipping organizations
for the AI era

Stacks and Stones Consulting exists to close the gap between technological possibility and organizational readiness. We believe that sustainable advantage comes not from buying AI, but from building the knowledge and capability to wield it effectively.

Our Approach

Education-first,
implementation-always

Every engagement combines structured learning with hands-on application. Our programs are designed so that knowledge sticks — because your team doesn't just hear about it, they build with it.

Depth Over Breadth

We go deep into each practice area rather than spreading thin. Mastery before scale.

Build to Transfer

Every engagement leaves your team more capable than when we started. Knowledge transfer is the deliverable.

Practitioners First

Our partners have built, shipped, and operated what they teach. No theory without practice.

Context Matters

No cookie-cutter programs. Every engagement is shaped to your organization's reality, constraints, and goals.
